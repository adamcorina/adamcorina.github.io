Train Solo

presentation site coming soon

A desktop training system for people who already know how to train.

Most self-directed lifters don't need motivation systems or generic plans. What they're missing is the layer in between - knowing what to do today, whether it's working, and when to change something. Train Solo tries to fill that gap with a transparent planning engine that reasons about your training the way a coach would.

Match Me

A compatibility profile you can carry across apps. Take an eight-minute quiz, get a code, put it in your bio. The idea: let people understand the dynamic before you even meet - as friends or something more. Built out of a genuine interest in psychology - and the feeling that most matching systems don't go nearly deep enough.

Covers attachment, conflict resolution, emotional needs, communication style, and relationship expectations.
